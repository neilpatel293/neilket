About

Neilket Patel is a general dentist currently practicing at Aura Dental in Regina, Saskatchewan, with expertise spanning oral surgery, endodontics, and fixed and removable prosthetics including CAD/CAM workflows. He holds a Doctor of Dental Medicine from the University of Saskatchewan and a Master of Education from the University of Ottawa.

Prior to dentistry, Neilket conducted bioinformatics and cancer research at The Hospital for Sick Children in Toronto, contributing to published work on pediatric brain tumours. This scientific foundation informs his analytical and evidence-based approach to clinical practice.

He is licensed to practice in both Saskatchewan and Ontario, and holds certifications in nitrous minimal sedation and Basic Life Support. Beyond clinical work, he is passionate about mentorship, education, and giving back to the community.
